California AI Laws
(Annotated)

A collection of annotated California artificial intelligence legislation

  • SB 53 - Transparency in Frontier AI
    Requirements for frontier AI developers to publish safety frameworks, report critical incidents, and protect whistleblowers reporting catastrophic risks.
  • SB 243 - Companion Chatbots
    Requirements for operators of companion chatbot platforms, including safety protocols, disclosures, and protections for minors.
